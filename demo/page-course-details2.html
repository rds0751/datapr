BA-LL.B

The BA-LL.B Course is offered by Aryans College of Law which is approved by Bar Council of India (BCI) and the University Grants Commission prescriptions.
At Aryans College of Law, we aim to foster a culture of scholarship and academic excellence amongst the Law student.
In our BA-LL.B Program, which is five years programme consist of 10 semesters, which enables you to earn a graduate degree in Arts as well as in Law, simultaneously over the duration of the course. Aryans College of Law BA-LL.B course has proved an ideal and best one for students who are willing to choose law as a profession. The curriculum of the BA-LL.B Course includes both practical as well as theory sessions.
Our Dual course Law Program is successfully luring student from all over states in India i.e Bihar, Haryana, Punjab, Assam and Jammu & Kashmir and presenting us as the best college for law education.
The students who were graduated from our department are shaping their future in the various law field.

Course Objectives

  • To Provide a deep Insight of Law Development
  • To understand political factors which are influencing Law policies
  • To elucidate the complex relationship between social and legislation Problems.
  • To solve social Problems of society.
  • To aim at Social sensitive sentinels of justice

    • Award

    Students who successfully complete the BA-LL.B Program are awarded the BA-LL.B degree by the Punjabi University, Patiala subject to University regulations.

    Age

    Age criteria should be met in accordance with the Bar Council of India (BCI). For more Info Visit www.barcouncilofindia.org.

    Course Information

    • Course Duration: 5 Years (10 Semesters)
    • Future Scope: There are plethora of opportunities for a law graduate. One can either practice as an advocate in a court of law or work with corporate firms. By clearing exams conducted by Public Service Commissions, a law graduate can become a judge. After gaining experience, a law graduate can hope to become Solicitor General, a Public Prosecutor or offer services to government departments and ministries. One can also work as a legal adviser, teaching in Colleges for various organizations.

    Eligibility

  • Minimum 50% or above in Intermediate
  • The score of the Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) will be given weight.

    • Course Requirements

  • Class 10th and 12th original marksheet attached with 2 sets of the photo.
  • School Transfer Certificate
  • School Migration Certificate
  • Passport size Photos (4)
