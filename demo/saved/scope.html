Scope of Law At Aryans College

Career In Law - An attractive profession with lots of money and wide scope.
It is the most honored Profession not only in India But also in many other countries. There are many great opportunities and a wide range of career possibilities. There has been a tremendous change in Law education in India for 15 years.
In Present Era, the students are interested in learning the law and practicing it because there are numerous career opportunities in it.
For a student who wants to build his career in law then they don't have to worry, there are plenty of opportunities in the field of law and it's not only limited to high courts, district courts or Civil court but Outside there are more opportunities like Corporate sector Jobs, administrative Jobs and many more.
The LLB graduates can choose his career beside legal services as government advocate, public prosecutors, members of law commission, solicitors, Attorney General of India, legal secretaries in assemblies, and income tax excise.

Career prospects for a law graduate:

Litigation:

Litigation Lawyer can also be termed as Prosecution. The work of Litigation lawyer is to fight the case in a court by hearing out the case and drafting it for the client.
Some of the Examples of Litigation officers are a Tax lawyer, Property Lawyer, Constitutional and Arbitrary lawyer.

Indian Legal service:

A top-rated legal service in India. If any law graduates want to make their career in Indian Legal service then he has to clear the UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) Exam.

Legal journalism:

There is also a trend of making a career in Legal journalism. In legal journalism, you can run an NGO, work with international organizations and many agencies.

Teaching & Writing:

Many law graduates in India are trying their luck in prestigious universities and law college for the post of professor and guest lecturers. Many of them are writing Journals, reports, books, and thesis and making their name.

Law firms:

There are numerous Law firms in India and many other countries. It is basically a Joint organization - Joint Organization of lawyers to work under one roof.
There are many advantages of joining any law firms such as high salary, luxurious office, diverse client base, and extensive client base.

Fields of Specialization in law are numerous such as constitutional, Administrative, Human rights, Intellectual, Business and Real Estate Law.

Here are some several job roles related to Law:

Property Lawyer, Corporate Lawyer, Consultant (Legal), Government Lawyer, Family Lawyer, Labor and Employment Lawyer
