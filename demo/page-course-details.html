B.COM-LL.B

Aryan College of Law offers B.COM-LL.B course, which is best for those students who want to pursue their career in Commerce field as well as in Law. Aryans College of Law offering you dual course program i.e. B.Com + LL.B, which is a five-year undergraduate program in which total 10 semesters are included. The B.COM-LL.B course is specifically designed to provide students with the knowledge of commerce as well as Law. It is an excellent combination in which the student will gain business background along with different laws vital in a professional world.

According to the present scenario, Legal education is gaining importance in various commercial sectors, so to shine in those fields we at Aryans are training our students in the best possible way.
The Dual Course i.e. B com LL.B offered by us is designed in a very specific manner according to BCI, which is very helpful in an understanding of the law in a variety of contexts. Aryans College of Law is the best and top educational hub for Law in India. The students form Various states (like Haryana, Punjab, Assam, Bihar, and Jammu Kashmir) fulfil their dreams by joining our college.

Course Objectives

  • To Build Self-confidence, Understanding and Knowledge of our graduates to serve society.
  • To deliver the best set of commercial and regulatory context knowledge to graduates.
  • To Make them capable of choosing the career in business and finance industry such as Legal Advisor, Attorney       General, HR Manager (Human Resource Management), Law/Assistant officer and corporate lawyer etc.

    • Award

    Students who successfully complete the B.Com-LL.B Program are awarded the B.Com-LL.B degree by the Punjabi University, Patiala subject to University regulations.

    Age

    Age criteria should be met in accordance with the Bar Council of India (BCI). For more Info Visit BCI Website.

    Course Information

    • Course Duration: 10 Semesters (5 Years)
    • Future Scope: Law as a profession is in high demand these days. There is a rising demand for the lawyers due to the changing social and economic circumstances and the ever-increasing regulatory roles being undertaken by the government. One also has an option to work as a civil lawyer, criminal lawyer, administrative lawyer, family lawyer, and a taxation lawyer. In our daily life, we may come across a situation where legal advice is required, in such a case, we need the advice of legal professionals who can analyze and interrupt law properly.

    Eligibility

  • Minimum 50% or above in Intermediate
  • The score of the Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) will be given weight.

    • Course Requirements

  • Class 10th and 12th original marksheet attached with 2 sets of the photo.
  • School Transfer Certificate
  • School Migration Certificate
  • Passport size Photos (4)
