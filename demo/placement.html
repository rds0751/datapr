Placement at Aryans College

Aryan college of law is one of the top colleges for placement. Every year a large number of students get placed in many financial and law-related corporate field like Technology, Medical, Legal, Management, District court and many more.
We are able to bring reputed recruiters year after year. The companies those come for recruitments have extensive faith in our students to play integral roles in their organizations.
We set a new record in Placement and Training. We have a strong corporate alliance which has led us to be the best law college in placement.
Approved by Bar Council of India, we are reckoned as one of the best law colleges in Punjab for legal research & teaching.
We craft professionals of future who has the passion toward work and most important is who can critically understand and judge the need of the moment. We craft specific skills and ethical values in our students.
