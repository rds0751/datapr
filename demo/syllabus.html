Aryans College of law is established in the year 2007 with a vision to provide global legal education to its students.

To achieve the vision to provide global legal education, Aryans as one of the top and best law college in India, started to appoint professional and experienced faculty members from all across the globe.

One of the best law college in North India that promotes excellence in teaching, learning, and research in law. We offer a three-year LL.B programme, five-year BA-LL.B (Hons.) and five-year B.Com-LL.B (Hons.) programme.
Our Law campus is beautifully designed and landscaped. Our campus has State-of-the-Art infrastructure and highly educated faculty to groom the students for extremely competitive fields of law.

We have a clear aspect of becoming an academically great institution responsive to the terms of the corporate world in this century.
We have developed uniqueness in luring students from all across India, who are trying to achieve their goals and make the career in the field of Law. We at Aryans believe in overall developments of student’s personality so that they reach their goal easily.
Along with the best quality of education, our department is designed to foster excellence in teaching, learning and research in law.

OUR MISSION:

Our Mission is to translate our vision into reality and here are some objectives that we want to achieve:

  • To continuously strive for research, development & innovation in law-related fields.
  • To provide the world-class facilities & ambience for students to showcase their talents & win
  • To provide an advanced level of teaching and practical training to students.
  • To develop students as responsible citizens with conscience, commitment & education to solve complex problems of society.
  • To encourage Industry-Institute Interface & promote basic development activities.
  • To have collaboration with leading Law Universities all over the world with a view to solve the complex problems of society.

OUR VISION:

Our Mission is to translate our vision into reality and here are some objectives that we want to achieve:

  • To be a top ranking Law Institute of choice for students and corporate.
  • To be known for superiority in Higher Education and Research especially relevant to social needs.
  • To overcome from the drawbacks, social responsibilities of society through our legal service program
  • To promote multidisciplinary inquiry
  • To create a practical appreciation of the socio-legal problems.

Course Duration:

Aryans Group of Colleges offers 3 Years (6 Semesters) and 5 years full-time Law course such as BA LL.B, BCom LL.B, LL.B which is approved by Bar Council of India (BCI).

Eligibility:

  • Graduated/Post Graduated or any equivalent examination by securing at least 50% marks in any stream.
  • Applicants age: Candidate Age should not be more than 30 years
