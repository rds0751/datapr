Law Courses

Aryans College of Law offers a three-year LL.B programme, five-year BA-LL.B (Hons.) and five-year B.Com-LL.B (Hons.) programme.

Modern Library

The Aryans College of Law Library has vast area and suitable for students to study at any time. We also have a Digital library.

Qualified Teachers

Aryans is one of the top and best law college in India, started to appoint professional and experienced faculty members from all across the globe.

About Aryans College of Law:

Aryans College of law is one of the best Law colleges in Chandigarh. The college is approved by Bar Council of India. The college provides the best law courses which are LLB, B.A-LLB, and B.COM-LLB. The Bachelor of Law is an undergraduate degree in law offered to students who wish to pursue their career in law and justice. B.A.LLB is a five years course, and the student can pursue it after passing the 12th exam. LLB is a three years course and its open for graduates.
